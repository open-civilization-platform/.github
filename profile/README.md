# Open Civilization Platform 🌍

> **"The world is now too small for anything but brotherhood."** — Jacque Fresco

The **Open Civilization Platform** is a digital framework designed to manage the world's resources, human needs, and industrial production through data and science, rather than money or politics. Inspired by the **Zeitgeist Movement** and **The Venus Project**, it aims to facilitate the transition to a **Global Resource-Based Economy (RBE)**.

## Vision
To create a "Civilization Operating System" that optimizes the planet's carry capacity while ensuring the highest possible standard of living for all human beings, without the need for debt, trade, or labor (through automation).

## The 7-Layer Architecture
The platform is organized into 7 functional layers:

1.  **[Biosphere Layer](docs/architecture/7-layers.md#layer-1-biosphere)**: Real-time monitoring of planetary health and raw resources.
2.  **[Resource Integration Layer](docs/architecture/7-layers.md#layer-2-resource-integration)**: Normalization and spatial mapping of available assets.
3.  **[Human Needs Layer](docs/architecture/7-layers.md#layer-3-human-needs)**: Scientific tracking of global human requirements (food, water, housing).
4.  **[Strategic Planning Layer](docs/architecture/7-layers.md#layer-4-strategic-planning)**: AI-driven optimization of production and resource allocation.
5.  **[Production & Automation Layer](docs/architecture/7-layers.md#layer-5-production--automation)**: Direct control and management of autonomous manufacturing.
6.  **[Distribution Layer](docs/architecture/7-layers.md#layer-6-distribution)**: Frictionless access-based logistics.
7.  **[Interaction Layer](docs/architecture/7-layers.md#layer-7-interaction)**: The global human interface for collaboration and scientific democracy.
